Our Story

Made with Passion,
Served with Love

Atwood Catering is a locally run catering business owned and operated by Brandon and Jen Atwood. What started as a love for bringing people together over great food has grown into a thriving catering service trusted by our community.

We specialize in beautifully crafted charcuterie boards, stunning grazing tables, custom platters, and full event catering. Every spread we create is made with fresh, quality ingredients and an eye for presentation that makes your event truly special.

Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a large celebration, we bring the same level of care and attention to every order.

— Brandon & Jen
